# The American Diatribe I'm an average guy. I rant about society and other shit that annoys me. I hope you enjoy. This page has some links to highlighted articles. Most articles will be linked in the relevant topic pages. I strongly believe in FOSS (free and open source software). I abhor the profit-seekers who exploit information and useful tools-which are effectively free to distribute-to build empires. This parallels the commodity markets where people consume far more than they need to live a comfortable life, partly out of brainwashing and partly out of plain ignorance. The average tech-illiterate American (but I repeat myself) goes about life paying for all sorts of services they don't need. Spotify Premium, Discord Nitro, YouTube premium. All completely insane payments for no real improvement. But I digress. My distaste for this sort of exploitation has spurred me on to use the MIT license.
